# Jaroor Fitness Website - Project Summary

## Project Overview
Successfully created a comprehensive, multi-page website for Jaroor Fitness, a unisex gym located in Bhilai, Chhattisgarh. The website showcases the gym's facilities, services, and membership options while providing an engaging user experience.

## Website Structure

### Pages Created:
1. **index.html** - Main landing page with hero section, featured services, testimonials, and call-to-action
2. **about.html** - Detailed information about the gym's history, mission, values, and member testimonials
3. **services.html** - Comprehensive overview of all fitness services offered
4. **gallery.html** - Photo gallery showcasing facilities, equipment, and member transformations
5. **trainers.html** - Profiles of certified trainers with their specialties and credentials
6. **membership.html** - Detailed membership plans with pricing and special offers
7. **contact.html** - Contact information, location map, contact form, and business hours

### Key Features:
- **Responsive Design** - Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Modern UI/UX** - Clean, professional design with intuitive navigation
- **Interactive Elements** - Smooth scrolling, hover effects, and engaging animations
- **SEO Optimized** - Proper meta tags, structured content, and semantic HTML
- **Cross-browser Compatible** - Tested to work across major browsers

## Technical Implementation

### Technologies Used:
- **HTML5** - Semantic markup and modern structure
- **CSS3** - Custom styling with Flexbox and Grid layouts
- **JavaScript** - Interactive navigation, form validation, and smooth scrolling
- **Font Awesome** - Professional icons throughout the site
- **Google Fonts** - Poppins and Roboto for typography

### Design Features:
- **Color Scheme**: Professional blue (#2563eb) and white theme
- **Typography**: Clean, readable fonts with proper hierarchy
- **Navigation**: Sticky navbar with hamburger menu for mobile
- **Animations**: Smooth transitions and hover effects
- **Layout**: Grid-based design for optimal content organization
