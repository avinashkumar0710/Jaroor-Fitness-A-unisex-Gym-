Cardio Training

Strength Training

A comprehensive weight room with Olympic barbells, dumbbells (up to 50kg), cable machines, Smith machines, and functional training rigs. Build raw strength, increase muscle mass, or train athletically — the tools are all here.

  • Olympic & powerlifting setup

Personal Training

Our ISSA and ACE certified trainers craft completely personalized training programs based on your goals, fitness level, and schedule. With regular progress assessments and program adjustments, you'll never plateau again.

  • Certified 1-on-1 coaching
  • Custom workout programming
  • Monthly progress assessments

Group Classes

High-energy group fitness classes led by passionate instructors. Zumba, power yoga, aerobics, HIIT circuits, and more — scheduled throughout the week so you can always find a class that fits your timetable.

  • Zumba & Dance Fitness
  • Power Yoga & Flexibility
  • HIIT & Aerobic Circuits
Included in Standard+

Nutrition Counseling

You can't out-train a bad diet. Our certified nutrition coaches create personalized meal plans, teach macro tracking, and provide ongoing dietary support to complement your training and accelerate your results.

  • Personalized meal planning
  • Macro & calorie tracking
  • Supplement guidance
